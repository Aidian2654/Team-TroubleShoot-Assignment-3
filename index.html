Senior Marketing/Sales Hybrid Role

We are seeking a senior marketing and sales director to lead and define Troubleshooter’s go to market strategy.

The type of person we are looking for is someone with lots of enthusiasm and very autonomous as you will be joining a small team and will be required to define how and where Troubleshooter fits in the market and the message we send out to customers. On top of the marketing strategy any new inbound leads are to be assessed and actioned. We are a small team at the moment but would envision you leading and hiring for the future marketing/sales team as we grow. We look forward to receiving your application!

Your profile:

  • Enthusiastic
  • A self-starter
  • Open to new ideas and willing to pivot on short notice

Technical skills:

  • Over 5 years in a marketing type role
  • Experience with the sales process
  • Preferably managed people before
  • Excellent written communications

For the right candidate Troubleshooter offers a competitive salary, monthly team outings and annual learning and development budgets.
